Mix veg

Mix veg is a healthy and flavorful vegetarian dish made with a variety of fresh vegetables cooked in a fragrant blend of spices and herbs. Enjoy it as a main course or a side dish!

prep time

15 min

cook time

20 min

servings

4 servings

Tags: veg veg veg veg

Instructions

Step 1

Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Add cumin seeds and saut√© for a minute until they start to splutter.

Step 2

Add chopped onions, ginger, and garlic to the pan and cook until the onions are soft and translucent. Next, add chopped vegetables such as carrots, potatoes, beans, peas, and cauliflower and mix well.

Step 3

Add spices such as turmeric, cumin powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt to taste. Mix well and cook for about 15-20 minutes until the vegetables are tender and cooked through. Serve hot with rice or bread. Enjoy!

ingredients

1 onion, chopped

1 large potato, chopped into small pieces

1 large carrot, chopped into small pieces

1/2 cup green beans, chopped

1/2 cup cauliflower florets

1/2 cup peas

tools

Cutting board - for chopping the vegetables into small pieces

Knife - for slicing and dicing the vegetables

Pan - for cooking the vegetables and spices

Spatula - for stirring the vegetables while they cook and to prevent them from sticking to the pan.
